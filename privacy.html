Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2025

Overview

The Minecraft New Tab Chrome extension is designed to enhance your new tab experience with Minecraft-themed visuals and interactive features. We take your privacy seriously and ensure that no personal information is collected, stored, or shared by this extension.

What Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or sensitive user data. The only data stored locally in your browser is:

  • Your selected biome (e.g., Forest, Desert, etc.)
  • Your day/night preference

This data is saved using Chrome’s storage API and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data with third parties.

Permissions Justification

The extension requests the storage permission only to remember your background settings (biome and time of day) between sessions. No other permissions are requested or used.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this privacy policy will be clearly posted on this page. You are encouraged to check back periodically for updates.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: support@minecraft-tab.dev
